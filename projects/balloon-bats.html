Balloon Bats

VR game with live users — internship project at Mayflower Worlds.

Overview

During my internship, I worked on Balloon Bats, a multiplayer VR game with real users. My work focused on monetization, multiplayer synchronization, and performance optimization.

Key Contributions

  • Rebuilt in-app purchase backend in C# + PostgreSQL
  • Fixed monetization blockers that had prevented revenue
  • Implemented multiplayer syncing for purchasable items

Impact

  • 195% increase in revenue per paying user (May → June)
  • Multiplayer purchases accounted for ~60% of revenue
  • Minigame assets used by 6,500+ players

What I Learned

  • Designing monetization systems that scale safely
  • Debugging distributed multiplayer state
  • Balancing performance with gameplay experience
