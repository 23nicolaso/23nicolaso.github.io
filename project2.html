About This Project

I love Cookie Clicker, and I love Gacha games. So I decided to combine the two! In Gacha Clicker, you try to get as many cookies as possible by clicking the cookie and buying cookie generators. However, unlike standard Cookie Clicker where you buy generators directly, in Gacha Clicker you get generators by rolling for them, and can only use up to 6 generators at a time, so you have to be strategic about it! In addition, there are set bonuses to be had by placing certain combinations of cards in your active deck, so there's a lot of depth to the game.
