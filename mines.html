Mines Project

When development on this area began, average playtime per session was low, so players rarely met or interacted in the lobby. To address this, I proposed and built the Mines â€” a challenging, repeatable activity players can do while waiting.

I developed the Mines solo, covering level design, 3D modeling, texturing, sound design, and animations. The feature now includes monsters, mineable ores that award in-game currency, and procedurally generated cave systems polished in Blender.


Key Challenges & Solutions

  • Procedural cave generation: 3D, low-poly cave generation has fewer established approaches than 2D. I combined a cellular automata generator with flood-fill region filtering and leveraged Blender modifiers to achieve performant, natural cave geometry.
  • Core PvE systems: I balanced rapid iteration for weekly patches with building new mine levels and creating monsters, ensuring combat and progression felt cohesive and shippable each week.

Impact: Average playtime per player more than doubled, even as the community scaled rapidly.
