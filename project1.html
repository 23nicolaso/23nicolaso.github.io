Ollama Market

A full-stack stock market simulation powered by Agentic AI

About This Project

Ollama Market is a simulated stock exchange with a single-threaded order matching engine and trading agents that trade off LLM generated outputs. I made this project to apply my learning from my data structures and algorithms classes, and experiment with the capabilities of small, open-source LLMs (1-8 billion parameter models)
